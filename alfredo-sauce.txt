Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients
1/4 cup butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp Pepper (or to taste)

Directions
Melt butter and roast garlic in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. 
Slowly add cream and whisk quickly for 3 to 5 minutes, heating through. 
Slowly add Cheese and whisk quickly until cheese is melted.
Stir in pepper, add salt to taste and serve over your choice of pasta.

Tip: Add chicken after pepper for an awesome chicken alfredo.
